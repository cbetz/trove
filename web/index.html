M4 · community benefit gap

The headline

Hennepin County Medical Center, Minneapolis. Reports $64.3M of charity care cost to CMS on Worksheet S-10 of its Medicare Cost Report. Reports $34.4M of financial assistance at cost to the IRS on Schedule H of its Form 990. Same concept, different filings, $30M apart.

That comparison didn't exist as a public, queryable dataset before this project. It does now — for any U.S. nonprofit hospital, every year — in four lines of Python:

hcris   = pivot_wide(parse_hcris("HOSP10FY2023.zip"))   # CMS S-10, by CCN
sched_h = parse_990("2024_TEOS_XML_01A.zip")             # 990 Schedule H, by EIN
cw      = load_seed()                                    # CCN ↔ EIN
gap     = community_benefit_gap(hcris, sched_h, cw)

Phase 1 datasets

  • CMS Medicare Cost Reports (HCRIS, form 2552-10) — parser + 44-variable dictionary shipped
  • IRS Form 990 Schedule H (hospital community benefit) — parser + 19 fields shipped
  • CCN ↔ EIN crosswalk for joining the two — 3,523 hospitals from CBI, shipped
  • Composed analytics — community_benefit_gap() shipped
